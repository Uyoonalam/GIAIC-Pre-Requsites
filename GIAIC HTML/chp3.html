Vitamin B Complex and Their Sources

  1. Vitamin B1 (Thiamin)

    2. Found in enriched grains and seeds.

  2. Vitamin B2 (Riboflavin)

    3. Found in whole grains, enriched grains, and dairy products.

  3. Vitamin B3 (Niacin)

    4. Found in mushrooms, fish, poultry, and whole grains.

  4. Vitamin B5 (Pantothenic Acid)

    5. Found in chicken, broccoli, legumes, and whole grains.

  5. Vitamin B6 (Pyridoxine)

    6. Found in fortified cereals and soy products.

  6. Vitamin B7 (Biotin)

    7. Found in many fruits and meats.

  7. Vitamin B9 (Folic Acid)

    8. Found in leafy vegetables.

  8. Vitamin B12

    9. Found in fish, poultry, meat, and dairy products.
